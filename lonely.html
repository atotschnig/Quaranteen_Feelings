LONELY

  • Join an online community. Be it a subreddit of your favourite comic book or a discord server for a fun video game, there is an online community where you can find like minded people to have fun with!
  • Go on a socially distanced walk or bike ride with your friends. Enjoy the fresh air and companionship. Get a change of scenery and spend time with the people you love the most.
  • Call people you have lost touch with. Be it family, friends, colleagues or acquaintances, reconnect to gain extra comfort and support. Every bit of love goes a long way!
