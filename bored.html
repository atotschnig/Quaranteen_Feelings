BORED

  • Be creative. Bake a cake, start a journal, learn an instrument or paint a potrait. Pick up a hobby, do what makes YOU happy.
  • Start composting. It is beneficial for the environment as it helps reduce waste, enriches the soil and increases biodiversity. Remove your boredom and go green at the same time!
  • Clean your room. Decluttering will help you relax and utilise your time efficiently.
