ANXIOUS

  • Don't forget to breathe! Try some yoga and meditation while staying home. It will help you keep up both your physical and mental health at the same time.
  • Take the time to write your thoughts down on paper. It will help you see more clearly.
  • Take your mind off of things by watching a funny video or doing something you enjoy. Don't forget to take breaks away from your phone!
